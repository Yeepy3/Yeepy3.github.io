Yubin Choi

I'm an M.S. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Seoul National University (SNU), South Korea, advised by Prof. Saewoong Bahk. I received my B.S. degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Yonsei University, South Korea. I am currently seeking opportunities to pursue a Ph.D. program.

At SNU, my research focuses on real-world communication systems, including power-line communication and drone-based communication networks, as well as neural network–based CSI feedback systems for beyond-5G MIMO beamforming.

Research Interest

Building upon my previous work in communication systems, I aim to explore multimodal integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) as well as human-computer interaction (HCI) and cyber-physical systems (CPS) for practical applications such as healthcare and wearable devices.
